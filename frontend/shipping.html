Returns — 30 Days, No Stress

We want you to love every Shine product. If you're not completely satisfied, return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Here's how it works:

1

Log in to My Orders

Access your account and visit My Orders to start the return process.

2

Select Items & Reason

Choose the items you'd like to return and tell us why.

3

Print the Prepaid Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return label instantly. Just print and attach it to your parcel.

4

Drop Off at Any Courier Point

Take your parcel to any registered courier point near you.

5

Receive Your Refund

Refunds are processed within 5 business days of us receiving the returned item.

Custom-made fragrances and opened skincare products cannot be returned for hygiene reasons unless faulty.
