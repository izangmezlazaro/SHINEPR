Returns

We want you to love every Shine product. If you're not completely satisfied, return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

How to return

  1. Log in to your account and visit My Orders.
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return and the reason.
  3. Print the prepaid return label we email you.
  4. Drop the parcel at any registered courier point.
  5. Refunds are processed within 5 business days of us receiving the item.

Custom-made fragrances and opened skincare products cannot be returned for hygiene reasons unless faulty.
